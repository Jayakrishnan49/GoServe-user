Features

Everything you need,
in one app.

GoServe brings the full service booking experience to your fingertips.

🔍
Service Discovery
Browse and discover home services near you. Find trusted local professionals quickly and book in just a few taps.
📍
Real-Time Tracking
Monitor your service request status live. Know exactly when your provider is on the way at every step.
📞
In-App Calling
Call your service provider directly from the app — no need to share personal phone numbers.
💳
Secure Payments
Pay safely via Razorpay with automatic bill generation after every service completion.
⭐
Ratings & Reviews
Rate and review providers after every service. Help build a community of quality professionals.
🔒
Secure Auth
Login via Google, Email or Phone OTP. Your data is always protected with Firebase Authentication.
